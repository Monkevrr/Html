UZG Remastered (Public Beta)

Download Now

WEBGL NOTICE:

Please fullscreen the game when it first starts up to fix any bordering issues. Also, change your render scale in settings to fix the pixelated graphics.

ANDROID NOTICE:

Mobile controls are currently super work in progress. They semi-function properly however you can plug in a controller and it should work fine.

UZG Remastered is a zombie survival shooter inspired by classic COD Zombies. 

  • Classic UZG fully remade from scratch!
  • UZGR is on an updated engine on Unity 6, taking advantage of its features.
  • Updates and Quality of Life, UZGR presents many new features and polishes old ones.
  • Leaderboards! UZGR has a built-in leaderboard that is global and cross-platform! Fight for the highest score.
    • Completely remade maps! UZGR goes back and polishes and finishes previously incomplete maps.
    • In the future a, Level Creator, you can now use an in-game level editor to create and share levels.
